    Quick start
  </h2>
  <p className="text-base text-gray-600 dark:text-gray-400 mb-8">
    Get started with BioRAM in three simple steps
  </p>

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Choose your assessment tool">
      BioRAM is available in two formats: Excel-based tools for straightforward assessments, or the Java desktop application for advanced features and multilingual support.
      
      Download the appropriate version from the repository and extract the files to your preferred location.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Select your assessment type">
      Determine which assessment you need to perform:
      - **Biosafety**: Evaluate biological safety risks in laboratory environments
      - **Biosecurity**: Assess security measures for biological materials
      - **Integrated BioRisk**: Combined safety and security assessment (2022 version)
      - **ChemSAM**: Chemical security assessment
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Complete the assessment">
      Open your chosen tool and follow the structured questionnaire. The tool will guide you through:
      - Facility and laboratory information
      - Agent characteristics and handling procedures
      - Risk mitigation measures
      - Automated risk scoring and reporting
      
      <Accordion title="View example output">
        The assessment generates a comprehensive risk report including:
        - Overall risk score (low, medium, or high)
        - Detailed analysis by risk category
        - Recommended mitigation measures
        - Compliance documentation
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
